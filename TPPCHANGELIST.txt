- The Bullet-Bill sound plays when shooting out of a cannon.
- I-frames have been removed.

- Every floor (except for WF dirt and SS snow) plays the default stepping sound (which has been made a bit louder). (code by Crustian).
- Mountain painting now features a small hill in the bottom-right corner, which can be seen in footage.
- Trees only make leaf particles appear when mario climbs down them.
- Trees have a better import which makes them darker, now matching footage.
- Entering the Bowser trapdoor fades into a circle and there's no warping sound when doing so.
- The warp object below that trapdoor has been lowered, now matching footage closely.
- When Mario exits a painting after dying, he immediately says "Haugh!" instead of saying it when hitting the floor.
- Mario now goes into idle immediately when stopping while holding an object.
- Mario's running animation plays earlier.
- Mario's pre tip-toe animation has higher priority than the regular tip-toe animation.
- Both the tip-toe and pre tip-toe animations' playing speeds are more dependent on mario's current movement speed.
- Pre tip-toe plays longer than a frame.
- If Mario gets up from stomach sliding while the stick is held, he goes into running immediately.
- Now there's 2 dive getup animations, one if mario gets up from a dive activated by pressing b, and another if he slides automatically from a slope.
- When Mario sidesteps at low speeds dust appears.
- Now Mario can jump from a crouch.
- Dive rolling has been fixed, now Mario only does a short hop if the stick is not being held.
- When bonking at a wall, Mario says "No!" when hitting the floor instead of "Haugh!".
- When Mario is in C-Up mode, he won't take damage from Bowser's landing attack.
- Bowser's shrinking speed has been tweaked to match footage more closely.
- Smoke from Bowser's bomb has been made opaque and stays a little longer before dissapearing.
- Skidding has been made less slippery.
- Analog Swimming has recieved a slight speed increase, and now there's particles when mario does it.
- Input window for jumping out of water is smaller.
- Swimstop doesn't play when entering water.
- Mario doesn't bob when swimming at the surface of water.
- Mario's height when at the surface of water has been lowered, now matching footage.
- Mario doesn't move after being hit underwater.
- Hoot now stops completely when touching a wall.
- Mario gets top speed immediately when exiting sidestep (code by Guestpiki).
- Vertical flamethrower object in Fire Bubble has been removed, as it's not seen at all in any footage of it's area.
- Bullies act like if they were punched when bumping into Mario. 
- Mr. I uses the MIPS hopping sound effect when shooting a tear, which isn't the exact sound used in sw95 but it's closer than the one used in the final game.
